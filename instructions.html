INSTRUCTIONS

Use left and right arrow keys to point the Voyager in desired direction.

Press spacebar to engage thrusters to move the Voyager.

Rotate spaceship to land on its engine.

Adjust thrusters to match planetary gravity to land safely on planet.

Land on planets to harvest resources, replenish fuel and oxygen.

CAUTION!

AVOID ASTEROIDS AND COMETS.

Keep track of your fuel and oxygen! If you deplete them before reaching Eden, you have failed humanity!
